About Us

Welcome to the Climate Sentiment Analysis platform. Our mission is to build a tool that accurately determines the sentiment of tweets related to climate change, categorizing them as positive or negative. Positive sentiment indicates support for climate action, policies, or awareness campaigns, while negative sentiment highlights concerns or resistance to these efforts. Understanding these trends is crucial for shaping effective climate communication, addressing public concerns, and reinforcing successful initiatives.

Our team is passionate about climate change and its impact on the world. Through this platform, we aim to raise awareness and contribute to the global conversation on climate issues.

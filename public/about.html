About Us

Welcome to the Climate Sentiment Analysis platform. Our mission is to provide insights into public sentiment on climate-related issues. We gather data from various sources and analyze it to present clear and actionable information.

Our team is passionate about climate change and its impact on the world. Through this platform, we aim to raise awareness and contribute to the global conversation on climate issues.

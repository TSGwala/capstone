About Us

Welcome to the Climate Sentiment Analysis platform. Our mission is to build a tool that can accurately determine the sentiment of tweets related to climate change, categorizing them as positive, negative, or neutral. This will help in understanding public perception and trends regarding climate-related topics.

Our team is passionate about climate change and its impact on the world. Through this platform, we aim to raise awareness and contribute to the global conversation on climate issues.
